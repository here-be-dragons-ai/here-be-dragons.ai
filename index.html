Here be dragons

A calm space for humans I trust: Builders, Operators, Founders, Engineers, Designers, Creatives. Friends with clear minds, thinking about technology and responsibility.

Here be dragons (Hic sunt dracones) comes from early cartography: a mark near the edge of the known world. It became a metaphor for terrain where the map grows thin, the risks are real, and curiosity only works with humility.

No spam. Only occasional updates about the initiative.
